## Morning After the Pulse
- The party—now formally known as **the Star Seekers**—awakens at **Lux Arcanium** following the previous night’s chaos.
- Rumors spread among students about the group’s heroics during the beast attack.
- Faculty repair damage from the pulse; all party resources are refreshed.
- The Star Seekers review **Professor Vorin’s notes**, confirming:
    - There are **five Harmonics** tied to the world’s stability.
    - The **first harmonic, Rhythm**, is associated with **Galatea** and its ancient star charts.
    - The order of restoration matters.
## The Letter of Authority
- **Arch‑Magister Valessa Dora** gives the party a sealed letter of introduction for:
    - **Elder Albus Miralion**, head of Galatea’s Grand Observatory.
- The letter grants the Star Seekers authority to investigate the Harmonic of Rhythm.
## Journey by Sky Ferry
- The party departs Lux Arcanium aboard a **Sky Ferry** bound for **Constelletica**, capital of Galatea.
- Along the way:
    - A red **pulse** expands from the **Savawell Thicket**, visibly weakening as it travels.
    - Air‑creatures avoid the pulse, hinting at its dangerous nature.
## Attack in the Clouds
- A **masked, flickering humanoid** appears beside the ferry, seemingly riding the wind itself.
- The figure warns the party to turn back and attempts to drive them off.
- Combat erupts mid‑air:
    - Quinlan is gravely wounded by **dark magic**.
    - The party fights back, slowing and damaging the attacker.
    - The mask is shattered and torn free.
- The attacker retreats, but not before his instructions were discovered:
> **“The rhythm must not be found.  
> Galatea must remain silent.  
> Delay the Star Seekers.  
> Stillness is mercy.”**
- The shattered mask bears a **five‑pointed symbol**, matching markings seen in Professor Vorin’s research.
## Implications of the Mask
- The wording and structure of the message strongly suggest that:
    - The masked figure is **academically trained**, possibly tied to **Lux Arcanium**.
    - There exists an organized group actively **preventing the restoration of the Harmonics**.
- This confirms the party is being **actively opposed**.
## Arrival in Galatea
- The party arrives in **Constelletica**, a city famous for its star observatories.
- Krish contacts a Druvian cousin, Pranav, at the embassy for guidance.
- They learn:
    - The **Grand Observatory** houses the most complete star records.
    - Masked figures visited earlier that same day asking about the stars.
## The Grand Observatory
- The Star Seekers meet **Elder Albus Miralion**.
- He confirms:
    - The **stars have been wrong** for roughly **a week and a half**.
    - The discrepancies are rhythmic and unprecedented.
- Crucial revelation:
    - The **Harmonic of Rhythm** is not merely symbolic.
    - It exists **physically beneath the Grand Observatory**, deep within the structure.
    - Galatea itself was built around this Harmonic long ago.
## Session End State
- The party has:
    - Confirmed the **reality and location** of the Harmonic of Rhythm.
    - Identified a hostile faction working to maintain “stillness.”
    - Arrived at the doorstep of their **first true objective**.
### **Cliffhanger**
The Harmonic of Rhythm lies **below** – but others are already hunting it.
