## Aftermath of the Harmonic
- The party exits the depths beneath **Galatea’s Grand Observatory** with the **Harmonic of Rhythm** in their possession (carried by **Krish**).
- **Elder Albus** confirms the Harmonic’s authenticity–and, crucially, that **the stars have shifted closer to their proper positions** since it was removed.
    - This is the first concrete proof that retrieving the Harmonics _actively repairs the world_.
## Next Destination: Samitra
- Strange light now glows unnaturally on the horizon toward **Samitra**, suggesting the next disturbance–or Harmonic–is there.
- Airship travel isn’t viable; the party joins a **caravan** heading south through **Dhruv**, led by Krish’s cousin **Harsha**.
- Rumors spread that the southern forests are unsettled and roads are **moving**–an ominous sign.
## A Message from the Shadows
- **Quinlan** discovers an assassin’s mark from the **Savawell Thicket**.
- He is intercepted by **Firnath**, a scarred assassin who had trained Quinlan when he was younger.
- Firnath warns:
    - The **Shattered Will** is acting _too openly_.
    - They are **not the true hand behind events**, only a distraction.
    - Someone higher is pushing or allowing them to move freely.
- Message left behind:
    > _“Activity exceeds pattern.  
    > Shattered Will is not acting alone.  
    > Watch who benefits from their noise.”_
## Villain Scene: The Muted Herald
- A cutaway reveals **Saradin** kneeling before the **Muted Herald**.
- Saradin is chastised for allowing the Harmonic of Rhythm to be claimed.
- The Herald makes their ideology clear:
    - **Stillness must be preserved through balance, not coercion**.
    - Saradin mistook exhaustion for faith.
- Saradin is sent to **observe the Astral Covenant**, not destroy them–for now.
## The Ruined Village
- Traveling through southern Dhruv, the caravan encounters a **silent, abandoned village**.
- No signs of battle–only decay.
- **Krish** recognizes ritual channels leading to the village’s **heart tree**.
- The heart tree is petrified, drained beyond recovery.
### Krish’s Vision
- Krish receives a vision of **Saradin**:
    - Pressuring the village elder.
    - Driving a spike into the heart tree.
    - Extracting its essence while insisting the people “must endure.”
- This wasn’t destruction by force–it was **extraction through ideology**.
- The **symbol of the Shattered Will** is carved into the stones around the tree.
## Through the Bladed Pass
- The party leaves the caravan and crosses the infamous **Bladed Pass** into Samitra.
- Razor‑sharp stone, screaming winds, and biting sand make the crossing dangerous.
- Through teamwork, preparation, and trust, they successfully cross without serious injury.
- At the far side, they find a **gold‑laced porcelain shard**–evidence that Saradin or another lieutenant passed through recently.
## **First Steps into Samitra**
- Heat is oppressive. The light ahead is **wrong**, too bright, too focused.
- The glow is identified as coming from **Shavalin**, Samitra’s capital.
- The party camps at the edge of the desert.
