## Character Introductions & Setup
- The group finalizes character names and backstories:
    - **Krish Two Fates** – coin‑flipping, ceremonial‑garbed warrior from Dhruv.
    - **Quinlan Ashwick** – edgy, agile character connected to the Savawell Thicket.
    - **Vien** – charismatic, social, and upbeat upperclassman from Lux Arcanium.
    - **Sabra Shield Dancer** – flashy, spear-wielding dancer from Samitra.

Each character arrives at **Lux Arcanium**, the magical academy where envoys from multiple kingdoms have gathered to investigate the world‑wide crisis known as **the Breaking**—when the sun stopped moving 20 years ago.
## Orientation & Calibration Combat
- The **Arch‑Magister Valessa Dora** welcomes all groups.
- Your party is grouped together; adjacent is another group including **Mira Ashwyn** and a brooding Dhruv native named **Kael** (who clearly recognizes Krish).
- Instructor **Ser Korrin** guides your group into a magical test chamber to assess combat capabilities.
### Combat Highlights
- The group battles **three training constructs** connected to a central pylon.
- Several players spectacularly miss their opening attacks.
- Once Quinlan lands a hit, the constructs activate.
- A red pulse sweeps the building, turning constructs hostile (white → yellow → red).
- The group fights back:
    - Sabra’s spearwork,
    - Quinlan severing cables,
    - Krish and Vien contributing attacks and magic.
- They ultimately **defeat the constructs**, and Quinlan destroys the pylon with a dramatic arrow pull.

Valessa confirms these red pulses are happening **more frequently**, always tied to strange activity around the **Savawell Thicket**.
## Crisis in the Courtyard
As the groups gather for debriefing, another red pulse hits:
- Statues crack,
- Lanterns burst,
- Screams echo outside the hall.

A **tentacle panther** appears and begins terrorizing students.
### Party Response
- Krish attempts to herd students through a door (fails repeatedly until he checks the doorknob).
- Sabra engages the beast directly.
- Vien uses magic to calm panicked crowds.
- Quinlan helps force the doors open and distract the creature.

Eventually:
- The beast is driven away,
- Students are ushered to safety,
- Valessa quietly pulls the party aside.
## The Missing Professor & the Big Discovery
Valessa reveals the professor **Alera Vorun** is missing.

The party investigates her trashed study:
- Papers everywhere,
- Glass shattered from lanterns after the pulse,
- Chalkboard scribbled with crossed‑out city names and **a star-shaped diagram** connecting the five great kingdoms.
### Found Document
The party finds a research sheet titled:
**“The Harmonic Fracture: A Study of Savawell Thicket”**
Major revelations:
- The Thicket is reacting to a **failure in the planetary engine stabilizer rig**.
- Entry into the Thicket requires aligning **five harmonic frequencies**:
    - **Rhythm**
    - **Heat**
    - **Wind**
    - **Memory**
    - **Light**
- These must be restored **in order**, each located somewhere in the world.
- Alera believed the first harmonic, **Rhythm** could be found in **Galathea’s ancient star charts**, at their capital Consteletica.
## Party Goals & Next Steps
- The Arch‑Magister requests the group prepare for immediate travel.
- She confirms their group calibrated as **Tier 1** (barely).
- She asks for their party name; the group decides on:
### ✨ The Star Seekers ✨

Their likely first destination:
**Galatea**, to recover the **Harmonic of Rhythm**.
