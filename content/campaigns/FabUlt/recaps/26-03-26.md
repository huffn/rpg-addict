## Return to the Grand Observatory

The Star Seekers reconvene with **Elder Albus Miralion** at Galatea’s Grand Observatory. He confirms:
- The **stars have been wrong** for roughly **a week and a half**, coinciding with the first red pulses.
- These changes are not random–something fundamental has shifted.
- The **Harmonic of Rhythm** is real, ancient, and **physically located beneath the observatory**.
- Galatea did not create the Harmonic; the city was **built around it** as a place of stewardship.
Albus reveals that access to the Harmonic is sealed by design and can only be opened at the proper time. When the moment comes, he performs a ritual, opening a hidden descent deep beneath the observatory.

---
## Descent into the Deep
The party travels far below the city via a long spiral stair and a concealed elevator. The stone gives way to strange slate walls veined with moving green lights, all subtly flowing in the same direction–toward the heart of the structure.

At the bottom, they reach an ancient chamber containing:
- A central platform,
- A pedestal,
- A glowing interface clearly tied to planetary machinery far older than Galatea itself.
Before they can act–

---
## Ambushes by the Shattered Will
**Saradin of the False Stars**, Galatea’s lieutenant of the **Shattered Will**, attacks alongside masked operatives.
- Saradin confirms the Shattered Will’s goal:  
    **prevent the Harmonics from being restored**.
- Their belief:  
    _“The world is still by design. Do not resist the Breaking.”_
- Saradin wields powerful dark energy and repeatedly uses teleport‑like flickering to escape death.
After a fierce battle:
- The Star Seekers defeat Saradin’s operatives.
- Saradin is driven off again, badly wounded and furious–now personally invested in destroying the party.
- His continued survival establishes him as a **recurring nemesis**.

---
## The Harmonic Interface
With the chamber secured, the party activates the ancient console. The interface requires **three correct harmonic inputs**, represented as five‑letter words.

Through experimentation and insight, the party successfully enters:
- **PULSE**
- **PHASE**
- **TEMPO**
These inputs stabilize the system.

---
## Claiming the Harmonic of Rhythm
When the final calibration completes, the **Harmonic of Rhythm** manifests.

As the party makes contact, they experience a shared vision:
- A vast planetary machine spanning the world.
- The Harmonics acting as stabilizing anchors.
- One Harmonic being violently torn loose–triggering the Breaking and the red pulses.
The truth becomes clear:
> The Breaking is not a natural disaster.  
> **Something–or someone–is dismantling the world’s stabilizers.**

As the vision ends, the ground begins to shake, signaling that removing or awakening the Harmonic has consequences.

---
## Additional Discoveries
Through investigation earlier in the day:
- The **Shattered Will** is organized into **five regional lieutenants**, one per major region.
- Above them is a shadowy figure known only as **the Muted Herald**.
- The faction’s numbers have surged in recent weeks, suggesting coordination and urgency.

Spiritualists also report:
- The world itself seems to **cry out in pain** moments before each pulse.
- The stars shift immediately after.
