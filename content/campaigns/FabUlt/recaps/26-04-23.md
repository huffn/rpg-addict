## Arrival in Samitra
- The party enters **Samitra**, crossing open desert toward its capital, **Shavalin**.
- The heat is oppressive, the light unnaturally bright, and sand seems to reflect a glow that wasn’t there before.
- A brief desert montage establishes:
    - Long days of travel
    - Careful water management
    - Local foods (“licky stickies”)
    - The growing sense that **the land itself is behaving incorrectly**
## Shavalin Under Strain
- Shavalin is tense and subdued:
    - Armed **Shattered Will** sentries patrol rooftops and water stations.
    - Water is rationed via **water chits**, tightly controlled.
    - Markets are quiet; people whisper instead of haggle.
- The party learns the water shortage became severe **around 2½ weeks ago**, before the Harmonic of Rhythm was recovered–aligning with the rise of red pulses and Shattered Will activity.
## Zara of the Still Dunes
- The party witnesses **Zara**, leader of the Shattered Will in Shavalin, addressing the populace:
    > _“Heat is hunger. Hunger must be disciplined. Samitra survives because it knows restraint.”_
- Her philosophy of enforced endurance resonates with a fearful population.
- Zara locks eyes with **Sabra** during her speech, marking her attention.
- It becomes clear Zara is operating openly, not covertly–an unusual move for the Shattered Will.
## Gathering Intelligence
### Krish – The Druvian Embassy
- Krish reconnects with his cousin **Raji**, who confirms:
    - Zara effectively controls the city.
    - The **royal family is alive but under house arrest**.
    - Springs have dried up; light seems to emanate from the desert itself.
- Shattered Will control coincides with the worsening water crisis.
### Vien – The Library
- Vien meets **Xander**, a former Lux Arcanium peer, now a scholar in Shavalin.
- Xander shares crucial insights:
    - Zara’s rhetoric mirrors **Lux Arcanium ethical treatises** on restraint.
    - Ancient **desert ruins** contain pre‑modern technology:
        - Water‑filtering vessels
        - Self‑powered lights
        - Devices that still function
    - One ruin stands out above all others: **the Great Ruin**, a massive metal structure deep in the desert.
- Xander warns:
    > _“Some people would rather control the answer than hear it.”_
### Quinlan – The Watcher in the Dark
- Quinlan finds another assassin’s mark and is intercepted by an operative of **Fernath**.
- He warns:
    - The Shattered Will is acting **too visibly**.
    - Zara is _allowing_ attention.
    - Stillness cults tighten control when they are **losing**, not winning.
- Message left behind:
    > _“Something strange is here.  
    > The temple is guarded by a gang.  
    > You are being watched.  
    > Stay in darkness.”_
## The Royal Family
- The party reaches the palace and is diverted to the **guesthouse**, where **Sabra reunites with her mother, Queen Sanai, and her sisters**.
- The truth is revealed:
    - Zara demanded an ancient **royal heirloom**.
    - The royal family refused and was placed under house arrest.
    - The heirloom is **a key**, not a weapon:
        - Required to access the ancient machine within the Great Ruin
        - Without it, the machinery rejects intruders
- Queen Sanai entrusts the heirloom to Sabra and gifts the party equipment.
- A **secret tunnel** allows the party to leave the palace unseen.
- Water chits are provided for the journey.
## Preparing for the Ruins
- Sabra calls in an old contact, **Dahlia**, securing **war camels** for desert travel.
- The party retrieves water from a guarded station using coordination, deception, and timing.
- Overheard Shattered Will guards reveal:
    - **Zara plans to move most of her forces to the Great Ruin in three days**.
## The Great Ruin
- The party reaches the ruin:
    - A colossal **metal mountain**, far larger than expected
    - Scavenger dig sites mark failed attempts to enter from the sides
    - True entrances lie **at the summit**
- Leaving the camels behind, the party climbs the scorching metal pathway.
- At the top, they are confronted by **Shattered Will sentries**.
### Combat
- A brief but intense battle ensues.
- The party defeats the guards, confirming:
    - The Shattered Will is actively securing the ruin.
    - Time is limited before Zara arrives in force.
