**Format:** 2–3 sessions (or 1–2 if brisk)  
**Goal:** Onboard players to identities, themes, Fabula Points, actions, checks, combat, inventory, and basic magic.  
**JRPG Tone Cues:** Awe-inspiring academy, rival squad, tutorial test turns real, mysterious relic reaction, nighttime sabotage, storm of soul-energy, and first field lead.

## Session 1 — Orientation & The Exam Gone Wrong
### Scene A: Arrival at Lux Arcanium
- **Beats:**
    - Warm welcome in the **Sunspire Courtyard**; banners of each kingdom flutter.
    - A short orientation by **Archmagister Valessa Myrr** (calm, luminous, pragmatic).
    - Introduce **Rival Team: The Luminaries** (polite rivals; competitive but not cruel).
- **Teach:** Social checks (Orator shines), using **Identity** to reroll checks (e.g., “Royal emissary” for protocol), brief on **Fabula Points**(award 1 for dramatic intro RP).
### Scene B: Calibration Trial (Tutorial Combat)
- **Setup:** In the **Aegis Arena**, Proctor **Ser Korrin Aegis** explains a controlled simulation to calibrate safety wards before fieldwork.
- **Twist:** A **Soulstream flicker** causes the constructs to overclock; the simulation’s conjured **Glyph Golems** begin to inflict _real_ harm.
- **Opponents:**
    - 2–3 **Glyph Golems** (see _Enemies_ below)
    - 1 **Warding Node** (targetable hazard; stabilizing it reduces golem DR)
- **Teach:** Round flow, actions, Guard/Defend, basic spells (MP), taunts/provoke (Fury), counter opportunities (Weapon Master), environmental targets.
- **Optional Mechanic Prompts:**
    - **Theme Invocation:** Ambition/Duty/Justice for advantage or FP; encourage “tell us how your theme manifests.”
    - **Bonds (proto):** Let a player declare a proto-bond (“I cover you!”) to gain +1 forward to a defend.
- **Outcomes:**
    - They succeed; faculty express concern—**this isn’t the first ward stutter**.
    - Reward: **Soul-etched Charm** (trinket; 1/session: +1 forward on a soul/ward-related check), +100 zeni each, 1 Fabula Point each (if used identity/theme).
### Scene C: Council Briefing Interrupted
- In the **Orichalcum Lecture Hall**, the Council outlines: the **Terminus**, engine legends, **Savawell Thicket** anomalies, and **The Broken**.
- **Interruption:** Alarm crystals chime—**an intruder** has entered a restricted archive **or** a **Soulstream storm** is forming over the campus.
- Lead-in: choose Hook 3 or Hook 4 below as your **Session 1 end beat**.

## Session 2 — Night Intruder or Soulstream Storm
Pick either as the main tutorial:
### Hook 3: Night Intruder — “Shadows in the Stacks”
- **Setup:** The Seekers spot a **masked figure** (or **rogue automaton**) in the **Aureate Archives** late at night.
- **Path A: Pursuit/Chase** across balconies and book-bridges.
- **Path B: Confrontation** among freestanding shelves (half-cover, falling tomes hazards).
- **Teach:**
    - **Skill Challenge — Chase:** DEX (acrobatics), INS (predict route), WIL (focus vs. fear aura), MIG (force doors). 5 successes before 3 failures.
    - Tight-space combat, improvised actions, teamwork.
- **Clues Left Behind:**
    - A **map shard** showing the **Savawell border glade**;
    - **Glyph notations** for a **fractured node**;
    - A **soul-lantern lens** (rare component, see **Field Kit**).
- **Reward:** 150 zeni each, bond opportunities (protected X during chase), faculty trust.
### Hook 4: Soulstream Storm — “When the Wards Sing”
- **Setup:** A **Soulstream storm** sweeps the **Sunspire Courtyard**: floating tools, luminous motes, frightened students.
- **Micro-Challenges (choose 2–3):**
    - **Rescue:** Calm a panicked familiar (INS + WIL).
    - **Stabilize:** Patch a ward ring (Tinker check; team can assist).
    - **Containment:** Defeat a **Flicker Beast** that emerged through a ripple.
- **Teach:** Mixed challenges; light combat; creative spell use; inventory points in action.
- **Clue:** Storm resonances match **Savawell Thicket’s** last recorded fluctuations.
- **Reward:** 150 zeni each, **Resonant Chalk** (consumable; +1 on ward-drawing checks).

## Session 3 — The Artifact & The First Lead
### Scene A: The Savawell Relic Reacts
- **Location:** **Aetherion Reliquary Lab**.
- **Event:** A fractured crystal lattice from **Savawell** **sings** when the Seekers approach, pulsing in patterns that **match their themes**.
- **Teach:** Theme invocation, **Fabula Point** awards for dramatic choices; **Identity** re-rolls on resonance checks.
- **Vision Option:** Brief shared echo of **pre‑Terminus** world (show a turning sky, watchers in the deep).
### Scene B: The Missing Professor
- Scholar **Professor Alera Vorun** (ethics & soul harmonics) is missing.
- **In their quarters:**
    - Notes on **collapsed nodes** under Savawell
    - A sketched **Chord Map** linking Savawell → The Broken → Deep Engine

# 6) ⚔️ Encounters & Challenges
> **Tuning Guidance:** Calibrate to **4 PCs at Level 5**. Start a bit easy to teach mechanics; escalate with environmental effects.
### Tutorial Enemies
**Glyph Golem (Training Construct)**
- **Role:** Frontline bruiser that teaches focus fire and hazard control.
- **HP:** ~40–60 | **Defense:** Moderate | **Magic Defense:** Low-mod
- **Attacks:**
    - **Heavy Slam** (melee): Solid damage; can mark a target.
    - **Glyph Pulse** (AoE line or small burst): small damage, chance to **Shaken** (tests guard/positioning).
- **Trait:** **Linked to Warding Node** (each destroyed node reduces golem DR by 1).
- **Teach:** Target prioritization; using Defend; status recovery.

**Flicker Beast (Soul Aberration)**
- **Role:** Skirmisher spawned in storms.
- **HP:** ~30–45 | **Def:** Low | **M.Def:** Moderate
- **Abilities:**
    - **Phase Slash:** Ignores light cover.
    - **Static Howl:** Fear/Focus check; teaches WIL saves.
    - **Blink:** Move-avoidance teaches positioning denial.
- **Teach:** Control, interrupts, and using team set-ups to pin it.

**Masked Intruder (Agent or Rogue Automaton)**
- **Role:** Fast striker or stealth opponent.
- **HP:** ~30–40 | **Def:** High | **M.Def:** Low
- **Moves:**
    - **Smoke Veil** (escape/obscure).
    - **Edge Combo** (single-target burst on isolated PCs).
    - **Disengage** (leads to chase).
- **Teach:** Chases, terrain, teamwork to corner.

### Hazards & Skill Challenges
**Warding Node Overload (Arena)**
- **Skill Challenge:** 5 successes before 3 failures
- **Checks:** INS (diagnose), WIL (hold line), Tinker (patch), DEX (avoid arc), MIG (drag cables)
- **Success:** Reduces golem defense; end stutter.
- **Failure:** Brief surge (AoE damage), golems gain +1 damage that round.

**Soulstream Storm Micro-Challenges**
- **Containment Circles:** Draw precise runes (Tinker/INS) while teammates defend.
- **Fleeing Students:** Calm/guide (WIL/Orator).
- **Loose Debris:** MIG to stabilize structures, DEX to dodge.

**Library Chase (Aureate Archives)**
- **Obstacles:** Collapsing stacks (MIG to clear), swinging bridges (DEX), predicting path (INS), bravery under pressure (WIL).
- **Rewards:** Clue fragments, FP for bold cinematic moves.

# 🧭 CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE (REVISED)
This structure pushes Savawell to **Act III**, and the early campaign becomes a **kingdom‑hopping investigation**, each region offering:
- A **Key Insight** about the Terminus
- A **Stabilizing Artifact** required for safe entry into the Thicket
- A **Story Revelation** about the world machine
The PCs must gather all **Five Harmonizing Components** before the Savawell Thicket becomes accessible.

---
# 🔑 THE FIVE HARMONIZING COMPONENTS
_(Rename to your taste — “Harmonics,” “Aethershards,” “Arc Keys,” etc.)_
### 1. **The Radiant Keystone** — The Light Aspect
A soul‑infused relic that reacts to daylight harmonics.  
Housed in the **Radiant Seat’s Vault of Dawn**.
### 2. **The Desert Heart Lens** — The Heat Aspect
A crystalline ancient artifact buried beneath Samitra’s dunes.  
Focuses soul energy through heat and pressure.
### 3. **The Aetherial Coil** — The Wind Aspect
A piece of ancient machinery recovered from the peaks of Voland Etherium.  
Hums with high‑altitude soul frequencies.
### 4. **The Shadow Archive** — The Memory Aspect
A forbidden Dhruvian tome containing recollections of pre‑Terminus forests.  
Teaches how to walk the “paths between roots.”
### 5. **The Galatean Star Map** — The Rhythm Aspect
A chart of sky movements before the world stopped.  
Needed to predict soulstream pulses inside Savawell.
### **Combine all five → “Savawell Access Rite”**
This is the ritual needed to enter the Thicket _alive_.

- PCs must navigate religious expectations and political agendas.
### JRPG‑Style Beats
- Holy trial (like FFIV’s Paladin trial).
- A betrayal inside the clergy.
- Keystone reaction when PC touches it (theme-based resonance).
### Why it matters
The Keystone supplies the **light‑harmonic** needed to stabilize vision and truth in the Thicket’s hallucination zones.

### Story Hooks
- Samitra’s sands hide **old machine bones**.
- A buried ruin shows signs of a soulstream conduit.
- Desert storms vibrate like heartbeat rhythms (Terminus echoes).
### JRPG Beats
- Sand temple puzzle dungeon
- A desert guardian spirit
- A collapsing ruin escape sequence
- Moral dilemma with Samitran politics (resource scarcity)
### Why it matters
